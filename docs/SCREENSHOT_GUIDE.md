# Accounts HQ — Screenshot / Manual Capture Guide

A step-by-step capture list for the user manual, built around the seeded demo
company **“Sharma Trading Co.”** (a Delhi trader). Open Accounts HQ, pick
**Sharma Trading Co.** from the company list, and walk the screens in the order
below. Each entry says how to reach the screen, what the demo data shows, and a
suggested caption.

> **Demo at a glance** — FY 2026-27, 34 vouchers across Apr–Jun 2026.
> Headline figures to expect (so you can confirm the screenshot is the right one):
>
> | Figure | Value |
> |---|---:|
> | Sales (Goods) | ₹5,29,000 |
> | Purchases (Goods) | ₹3,70,000 |
> | Output GST | ₹65,160 |
> | Input GST (ITC) | ₹68,180 |
> | HDFC Bank balance | ₹5,55,040 Dr |
> | Cash in hand | ₹5,000 Dr |
> | Receivables (4 customers) | ₹2,40,460 |
> | Payables (3 suppliers) | ₹2,70,580 |
>
> Customers: Verma Electronics (Delhi), Mumbai Traders, Bengaluru Gadgets,
> Chandni Chowk Retail.  Suppliers: Delhi Wholesale Supplies, Surat Textiles,
> Noida Components. GST is a mix of **CGST+SGST** (Delhi parties) and **IGST**
> (out-of-state), at 5 / 12 / 18 / 28 %.

### Notes for the writer
- Screens marked *(if shown)* / *(plan-dependent)* depend on the licence on
  this machine. If a screen is **locked**, it shows an upgrade panel — either
  capture that (to show the upsell) or skip it.
- Shoot at a consistent window size (maximise the window) so all images crop
  uniformly.
- The demo book is balanced and self-consistent, so any figure you see ties back
  to the table at the top — handy for spotting a wrong-screen capture.
- To reset the demo to a clean state at any time, re-run:
  `python tools/seed_demo_trader.py --data-dir "%APPDATA%\AccGenie"`
