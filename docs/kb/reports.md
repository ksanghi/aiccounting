# Reports — view, filter, drill-down, export

*(Most reports are on the higher plans; Day Book and Ledger Balances are available free.)*

## Always-free views
- **Day Book** — every voucher in a date range; filter by type or search the narration;
  edit/delete from here.
- **Ledger Balances** — the running balance of every ledger.

## Financial reports
- **Trial Balance** — opening, debits, credits and closing per ledger, as of a date.
- **Profit & Loss** — income vs. expenses and net profit.
- **Balance Sheet** — assets vs. liabilities.
- **Cash Book / Bank Book** — cash and bank movements with a running balance.
- **Ledger Account** — every voucher for one ledger (the drill-down target).
- **Receipts & Payments** — summary across ledgers.
- **Receivables / Payables Aging** — who owes you / whom you owe, in buckets (0–30, 30–60,
  60+ days).
- **Cash-Flow Planning** — a forward cash view based on aging *(higher plan)*.
- **Bill-wise Outstanding** — which bills are settled / outstanding *(higher plan)*.
- GST and TDS reports — see the *GST* and *TDS* articles.

## Using a report
1. Set the **From/To** dates (or the single **As of** date).
2. **Filter rows…** — type to narrow the table instantly.
3. **Drill down** — click an account name in Trial Balance / P&L / Balance Sheet to jump to
   its **Ledger Account**; double-click a line in a register to open the **voucher** itself.
4. **Export** — **⬇ Excel**, **⬇ PDF**, or **Print**. (Email a report where offered.)

## Tip
Reports read your posted vouchers live — post or fix an entry and just **Refresh** the
report to see it update.
