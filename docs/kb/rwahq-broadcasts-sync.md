# RWA HQ — Broadcasts, joining residents and cloud sync

## Broadcasts / official channel
Broadcasts let the society — or an approved vendor — send a message to everyone through an official channel, with consent and billing handled. This is separate from resident-to-resident chat.

## Joining residents
Invite residents with a **join link** and your society's **HQ-ID**; a light **soft-KYC** step confirms who they are.

## Cloud sync
The desktop app is the source of truth. Resident-facing data (units, members, notices, dues) syncs to the cloud so the mobile app and resident portal stay current. Your accounting books stay on your PC.
