# RWA HQ — Maintenance billing, dues and collection

Raise maintenance bills against units, record receipts as residents pay, and track who is in arrears.

Residents can pay through your society's **UPI ID** (set in Society Settings) — the money goes **straight to the society's account**; RWA HQ never holds or routes it. Processing a resident payment is free.

Because billing is backed by full accounting, you get proper ledgers, aging and statements for dues — not just a list. See the RWA Reports and the Accounts HQ reports articles.
