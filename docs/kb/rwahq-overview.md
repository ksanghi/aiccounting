# RWA HQ — What it is and how it's used

RWA HQ runs a residents' welfare association or apartment/plot society. It has two parts:

- **The desktop app** (Windows) — the office/manager side: members and units, maintenance billing and dues, notices, complaints, visitor passes, broadcasts, and full society accounting. Your data lives on your own PC.
- **The resident mobile app** (free, iOS/Android) — what residents use: notices, complaints, chat with neighbours, visitor passes, and paying their dues.

Resident-facing data syncs securely to the cloud so the mobile app and resident portal work; the desktop app is the source of truth.

RWA HQ **records and reports** — it is not a statutory filing or compliance engine. For tax questions, defer to your CA.

RWA HQ is built on the same accounting engine as Accounts HQ, so all the accounting screens (vouchers, GST, reports, reconciliation, backup) work the same way.
