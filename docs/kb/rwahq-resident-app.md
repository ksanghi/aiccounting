# RWA HQ — The resident mobile app

Residents install the free **RWA HQ** app (iOS/Android). It gives them:
- Notices and complaints
- **Chat** — one-to-one and group chats with neighbours, without sharing phone numbers, plus calls
- **Visitor passes** from their phone
- Their **maintenance dues** with UPI payment

The app **updates itself** — residents don't reinstall to get new features. New residents join via a society **join link** and the society's **HQ-ID**, with a light soft-KYC step to confirm identity.
