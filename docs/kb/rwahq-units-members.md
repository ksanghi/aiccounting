# RWA HQ — Units (Flats/Plots) and members

## Flats / Plots
The Flats (or Plots) screen is your register of units. Each unit has an owner/resident and its own maintenance ledger, so its dues and receipts feed straight into the accounts. The screen's name follows the Flats/Plots choice made in setup.

## Members
The Members screen lists owners and residents with their contact details and their access to the resident mobile app. Keep it current so notices, bills and app access reach the right people.
