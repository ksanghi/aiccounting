# Settings, plans & period locks

## Settings
Open **Settings** from the sidebar. Common preferences:
- **Debit/Credit label style** — *Natural*, *Traditional*, or *Accounting*. Changes the
  Dr/Cr wording across the whole app.
- **Default voucher date** — *Today* or *Last-used* (the "sticky date").
- **After-post confirmation** — show/hide the "voucher posted" toast.
- **Financial year start** — default 1 April (India).
- **Company tax IDs** — GSTIN and TAN.
- **AI / Anthropic key** — your own key, required for the AI Document Inbox / Reader (BYOK).
- **Backup reminder interval** — how often to nudge you to back up.
- **Bank reconciliation: ask for a comment when ignoring a line** — for the audit trail.

## Plans & activation (License & Plan screen)
Accounts HQ runs on a plan. Roughly:
- **Free** — day-to-day entry and basic views.
- **Standard** — full financial reports, bank/ledger reconciliation, backup, migration.
- **Pro / Premium** — GST & TDS reports, AI Document Inbox/Reader, bill-wise, cash-flow
  planning, more users.

*(See the website Pricing page for the current plan limits and prices.)*

**To activate:** open **License & Plan**, paste your licence key, and save. Locked features
show an **Upgrade** card until your plan includes them. After changing plans you may need to
restart the app to unlock features.

**Offline:** the licence is cached on your PC and keeps working offline for a grace period;
it re-checks with the server in the background when you're online.

**Transactions counter:** your plan includes a number of transactions; each posted voucher
counts toward it.

## Period locks
Open **Period Locks** to **lock a date range** so no one can post, edit or cancel vouchers in
it — useful after you've closed and filed a period. Locked-period entries are blocked at the
moment you try to post or edit. Remove the lock to make changes again.
