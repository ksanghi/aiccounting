# tradeHQ — Overview

tradeHQ is a **trading & investment consolidation cockpit** for Windows. It
pulls reports from a family's many broker accounts and shows one consolidated
view: total wealth, cash position, realized profit/loss, ROI by trade type,
asset allocation, and profit over time (by day, week or month).

Who it's for: families and individuals who hold accounts across **several
brokers** and want one combined picture instead of logging into each broker
separately.

Key points:
- **Multi-broker:** consolidates holdings, P&L and ledger/cash flow across all
  your accounts, grouped by family member.
- **Auto-pull:** tradeHQ logs in to each broker for you (using your saved login
  and an authenticator/TOTP code it generates) and fetches the latest data —
  no manual downloads. Zerodha is supported today; more brokers are added over
  time.
- **Private & local:** your data and broker logins stay encrypted **on your own
  PC** (Windows DPAPI) and are never uploaded.
- **Bridges to Accounts HQ:** cash flow and holdings can flow to your Accounts
  HQ books for reconciliation.

tradeHQ records and consolidates your trading data — it is **not** a tax-filing
or compliance engine. For tax questions, check with your CA.
