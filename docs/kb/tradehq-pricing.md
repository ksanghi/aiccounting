# tradeHQ — Plans & pricing

tradeHQ has a simple two-tier model:

- **Free** — 1 broker account, manual pull, the dashboard and holdings view,
  1 family member. Good for trying it out.
- **Standard — Rs. 200 / month** — all brokers with **auto-pull** (TOTP login),
  the full family wealth view, asset-allocation, the AI news screener, the
  Accounts HQ cash-flow bridge, and up to 6 family members.

It runs on Windows, and your data stays on your own computer. Prices are in INR.
For the latest plan details, see the tradeHQ page on the website or ask us at
info@ai-consultants.in.
