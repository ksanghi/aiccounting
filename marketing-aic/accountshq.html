GST and TDS, worked out for you

State codes decide CGST+SGST vs. IGST on their own. TDS sections apply themselves above the threshold. Every figure stays traceable and every ledger export is audit-ready — your accountant will thank you.

  • All standard GST rates (0%, 5%, 12%, 18%, 28%)
  • TDS sections 194A, 194C, 194I, 194J, 194Q built in
  • HSN/SAC capture per item
  • Audit-ready ledger export
