How to reach us

Accounts HQ, RWA HQ, and tradeHQ are shipped on an "as is, where is" basis. We don't run an email support desk — please read this page before writing in.

For help with the Software: use the Feedback button inside the app. It captures your version, settings, and (optionally) a screenshot in one click, and lands in our triage queue. This is the fastest path — and the only one that reaches us reliably.

Where to send what

Bug reports

Use the in-app Feedback button. It attaches diagnostics automatically — much higher chance of a fix than describing the bug by email.

In-app → Feedback → "Report a bug"

Feature requests

Same place. Use the in-app Feedback button and choose "Suggest a feature". Your request goes onto the public roadmap queue. Popular ones get built first.

In-app → Feedback → "Suggest a feature"

Pre-purchase questions

Try the free tier or demo first — every paid feature is visible there. If that doesn't answer it, the Pricing and Terms pages cover scope, limits, and renewals.

No sales team. Evaluate via free tier.
