What we stand for

We build for the businesses automation usually skips — by doing a few things deliberately differently.

🎯

Built for one job, not a thousand

Each app does a single domain deeply, so it works the day you install it — no month-long setup, no consultant, no 200 settings you’ll never touch. Generic software makes you fit its way of working; ours fits yours, out of the box.

👌

So simple it needs no manual

If your team can use WhatsApp, they can use our apps. We design for the person at the counter, not the IT department — clear screens, sensible defaults, nothing to “learn”.

AI doing the boring work

We put AI exactly where it earns its keep — reading a bill, drafting an entry, catching a mismatch — so you review and approve instead of typing. The machine does the grunt work; you stay in control.

We rethink the workflow, not just digitise it

Most software just moves your paper process onto a screen. We question every step, drop the ones that waste your time, and use technology the efficient way — so the work actually gets lighter, not just computerised.

Priced to change the maths

We’re not chasing “cheapest on the list” — we’re making automation cost so little that a Micro or Small business finally says yes. Start free, stay free until it’s clearly worth more, then pay only the difference. No per-seat traps, no surprise bills.
