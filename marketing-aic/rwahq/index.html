How it works

One hub. Everyone connected.

Snap, scan or email a vendor bill and the AI drafts the entry for you. The committee runs the society on one computer, while residents, the guard at the gate and visitors all reach it — through the free app, WhatsApp or Telegram. The cloud is used only when you need AI, a payment or a licence check, and your data stays on your machine.
