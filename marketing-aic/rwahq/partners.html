Questions

The essentials before you apply.

How do partners earn?

You earn a share of the communication and advertising revenue generated within the network of societies you bring onto RWA HQ. The more societies and households in your network — and the more advertiser demand — the more it can generate.

Do I need advertising experience?

No. Your role is to help societies adopt RWA HQ and grow your local network. Advertising demand and pricing are managed centrally.

Do I need software experience?

No. RWA HQ runs the platform. You focus on relationships with societies in your city.

How are societies attributed?

Societies you onboard remain attributed to you, subject to the partner program terms — so your effort is protected and not duplicated by another partner.

Can multiple partners work in one city?

A city can support more than one partner, but each society is attributed to the partner who brings it on, under the program terms. We work with you on territory.

How are residents protected from spam?

Message limits are built in and adjust by community segment. Premium communities see fewer messages; the resident experience always comes first. No spam.

What message formats are supported?

Text, image and video messages. Richer formats carry higher rates; video is charged per second. Formats and pricing are managed centrally.

How often can advertisers send messages?

Within central limits that protect residents and vary by community segment — balancing advertiser reach with a clean resident experience.
