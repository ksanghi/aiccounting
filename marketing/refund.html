Cancellation & Refund Policy

Last updated: 26 May 2026

This policy applies to all paid subscriptions of Accounts HQ, RWA HQ, and tradeHQ published by [BUSINESS NAME]. The Software offers a free tier and / or a demo allowance so you can evaluate it before paying.

7-day refund window. If you find the paid Software is not right for you, request a refund within 7 calendar days of payment and we will refund the full amount, less any payment gateway fee that has been deducted by Razorpay and is not recoverable.

1. When you are eligible for a refund

  • The refund request is received at info@ai-consultants.in within 7 calendar days of the date of payment.
  • The licence has not been used to process a material volume of transactions, members, or trades. We consider "material" to be more than 100 vouchers / members / trades posted since the licence was activated.
  • The reason for the refund is genuine — for example, the Software does not work as described, a feature you needed is missing, or you mistakenly bought the wrong product or plan.

2. When refunds are not available

  • After the 7-day window has passed.
  • Where the licence has been used to post a material volume of records (as defined above) — the Software has clearly served its purpose.
  • For partial-period refunds inside a paid annual term other than the 7-day window — annual subscriptions are non-refundable beyond Day 7.
  • Where the licence has been transferred, shared, or used in violation of our Terms of Service.
  • Add-on credits and wallet top-ups (for example, AI credits or SMS credits) that have been consumed.

3. How to request a refund

  1. Email info@ai-consultants.in with the subject line "Refund request".
  2. Include the licence key, the email address used at checkout, and the reason for the refund. A short description of what didn't work for you helps us improve.
  3. We will acknowledge within 2 business days and confirm eligibility within 5 business days.

4. How refunds are processed

Approved refunds are returned to the original payment method via Razorpay. The refund typically appears in your account within 5–10 business days, depending on your bank or card issuer. Where the original payment method is unavailable (closed account, expired card), we will arrange a refund to a substitute method you nominate.

On approval of a refund, your licence key is deactivated and you must uninstall the Software.

5. Cancelling a future renewal

Renewals are not automatic — see Clause 2 of our Terms of Service. To stop receiving renewal reminders, reply to the renewal email or contact us and we will remove you from renewal communications.

6. Disputes

If you believe a refund request has been declined unfairly, you may escalate by emailing info@ai-consultants.in with the subject "Refund dispute". Unresolved disputes are subject to the jurisdiction specified in our Terms of Service.
